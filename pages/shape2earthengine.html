Shape2Earth Engine

Shape2Earth Engine is a standalone application that converts shapefiles to KML.

Shape2Earth Engine

Shape2Earth Engine is like a mini GIS that works in Google Earth, allowing you to explore data patterns in your spatial data.

Shape2Earth Engine makes it easy to:

  1. Convert Shapefiles to KML - Load a shapefile into Shape2Earth Engine, and with a single click of a button, export the data into Keyhole Markup Language (KML) that can be directly loaded into Google Earth.
  2. Apply Thematic Symbology to Your Data - Create thematic and choropleth maps with your data using a 'Single Symbol', 'Unique Values', 'Equal Breaks', or a 'Continuous Ramp'. 
  3. Share Spatial Data - Email your KML for or put it on a server for the world to see in Google Earth, ArcGIS Earth, or on Google Maps!
