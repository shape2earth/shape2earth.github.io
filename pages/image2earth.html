Image2Earth

Simpy put, Image2Earth is the easiest way for you to access satellite imagery.

What is Image2Earth?

Image2Earth is an easy to use website that lets you zoom into an area of interest in Google Maps, and then export the imagery you see into a single, high resolution, georeferenced image that you can use in your GIS or CAD software.

Image2Earth data is ideal for:

  • Agricultural management
  • Precision Farming
  • Environmental Management
  • Land Use
  • Assessment and Taxation
  • Engineering
  • Construction / Surveying
  • Rural Planning and Development
  • Urban Planning and Development
  • Cultural Resource Management
  • Real Estate / Property Management
  • Telecommunications
  • Transportation
  • Utilities
  • Education
  • Emergency / Disaster Management
  • Hazard Insurance
  • Public Health
  • Defense / National Security
  • Law Enforcement
  • Oil and Gas
  • Mineral Exploration

How Image2Earth Works

If you can pan and zoom in Google Maps, then you can use Image2Earth.

Navigate to your view

Search for your location, or simply navigate to it in the Image2Earth website. You can also drag a box over your are to be more precise.

Learn more

Export

At the click of a buton, your view is exported as a georeferenced JPEG image that you can use in the software of your choice.
